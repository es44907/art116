Resources

Explore a variety of resources designed to support language development for children with speech delays.

For Parents

  • Language Learning Apps: Try Duolingo Kids and Endless Reader for fun and interactive learning.
  • Printable Flashcards: Download our free vocabulary flashcards for extra practice.
  • Daily Conversation Tips: Encourage your child to speak by using simple phrases and positive reinforcement.
  • Storytime Recommendations: Check out our list of bilingual storybooks for engaging language learning.

For Teachers

  • Lesson Plans: Download structured lesson plans to teach Portuguese, Spanish, and English.
  • Interactive Activities: Use games and group exercises to make learning fun and engaging.
  • Classroom Posters: Display language charts to reinforce vocabulary and grammar.
  • Videos and Audio Resources: Access educational videos and audio lessons for additional support.
